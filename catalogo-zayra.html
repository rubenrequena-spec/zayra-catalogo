The Story Behind
Zayra

Zayra was born from a simple conviction: Spain produces some of the finest food and cosmetics in the world — and the UAE deserves access to the very best of it. We are four partners united by a deep respect for craftsmanship, provenance and quality without compromise.

Our Mission

From the sun-scorched groves of Jaén — where the world's finest extra virgin olive oils are pressed at dawn from hand-harvested olives — to the wild Atlantic coast of Galicia, where master artisans preserve the ocean's bounty without a single additive. From the ancient soils of Extremadura, where centuries of olive cultivation gave birth to a cosmetic tradition as pure as the land itself, to the legendary vineyards of La Rioja, where every bottle carries the soul of a terroir unlike any other on earth.

We seek only those producers who refuse to compromise — men and women who have devoted generations to perfecting their craft, who measure quality not in volume but in excellence.

The Emirates is not an ordinary market. It is a place where standards are extraordinarily high, where consumers are cosmopolitan, educated and uncompromising in their pursuit of the finest things in life. It is precisely for this reason that we chose it. The UAE deserves nothing less than the best Spain has ever produced — and that is exactly what we deliver.

"From the finest corners of Spain —
to the most discerning tables in the world."
